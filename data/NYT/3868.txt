

Canada will revoke the Canadian citizenship of 1,800 people who the government believes used fraud to obtain their status. Jason Kenney, the immigration minister, told a news conference on Wednesday that police and immigration investigators had found that most of the people had hired &ldquo;crooked immigration consultants&rdquo; to produce fake proof of residency in Canada. There has been a public debate about people who live in troubled parts of the world taking Canadian citizenship as a kind of insurance policy.
