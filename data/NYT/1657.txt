

Thousands of people marched through Mali&rsquo;s capital to protest the Western-led military intervention in Libya and show their support for Col. Muammar el-Qaddafi. The crowd marched to the French Embassy and then to the United States Embassy, yelling &ldquo;Down with Sarkozy! Down with Obama!&rdquo; Mali has close relations with Libya. Libya&rsquo;s government has invested heavily in Mali, and Colonel Qaddafi has financed the construction of mosques across the country.
