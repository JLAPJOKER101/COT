

Hundreds of students crowded into a courtroom in the capital, Ouagadougou, for the start of a trial of three police officers on charges of aggravated assault in the death of a student, Justin Zongo, while in police custody in late February in Koudougou. The government said he died of meningitis, but Mr. Zongo&rsquo;s fellow students said the death was the result of mistreatment and began protests that spread across Burkina Faso and left six people dead. The unrest spread to the armed forces with a mutiny that threatened the 24-year rule of President Blaise Compaor&eacute;.
