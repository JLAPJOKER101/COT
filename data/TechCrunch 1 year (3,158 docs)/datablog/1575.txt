No, silly, this isnt about murder  its a program to remove people from a photo! Scalado has created a photo-taking system that allows you to selectively remove people in a photo. How does it work? It basically interpolates the clean version of the scene by watching the moving, live objects. Its not rocket science, but its pretty cool.Theyll be showing a working version at MWC in Barcelona this year  a major telecom event  and it should launch for iOS and Android soon.
