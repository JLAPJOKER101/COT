

Seventeen police officers and forest officials have been convicted of gang raping a group of tribal villagers in the southern state of Tamil Nadu in 1992. The Press Trust of India reported that 100 other officials were found guilty on Thursday of brutally beating dozens of people from Vachathi, a village of dalits, also known as untouchables. The officials had raided the village to search for smuggled sandalwood and ended up raping 18 women and beating 100 people, reports said.
