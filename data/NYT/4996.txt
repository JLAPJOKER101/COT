

The chief suspect in the bombing of a popular Marrakesh cafe in April recanted his confession on Thursday and denied any involvement in the attack that killed 17 people. &ldquo;I have never set foot in Marrakesh,&rdquo; the defendant, Adel Othmani, told the judge in the trial. He said he had confessed under threat of torture. Mr. Othmani, one of eight defendants being tried in the attack, has been described as the main perpetrator.
