According to the Kickstarter API blog, a bug caused 70,000 unlaunched projects to be publicly visible over the weekend, allowing folks to see goals, funding plans, and descriptions on projects that havent yet appeared on the site. Of the 70,000, visitors only viewed 48.The bug exposed no financial information.Kickstarter fixed the problem on Friday, May 11 at about 2pm. It had been introduced into site code on April 24.The best thing?
