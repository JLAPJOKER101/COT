

The African Union said Monday that three people were killed when a suicide bomber and other militants attacked a peacekeeping base in Mogadishu. Two of the union&rsquo;s soldiers and one fighter from a government-allied militia were killed and five soldiers were wounded in the attack, the group said. Capt. Prosper Hakizimana of the African Union said that the bomber was shot before the blast and that two other attackers were shot and a third was captured. An Islamist militia is battling the government for control of Mogadishu.
