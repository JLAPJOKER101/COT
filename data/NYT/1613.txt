

Protestant and Catholic leaders in Northern Ireland&rsquo;s unity government celebrated their first full four-year term in power on Wednesday and lauded Ian Paisley on his effective retirement day. Mr. Paisley, 84, left, who spent decades rallying pro-British Protestants against compromise, stunned the world in 2007 by agreeing to forge a coalition alongside senior Irish Republican Army veterans.
