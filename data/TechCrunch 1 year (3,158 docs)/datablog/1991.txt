HackFwd, the pan-European accelerator created by Lars Hinrichs, the founder of LinkedIn competitor XING, recently held its open PitchInBerlin session, where startups are invited to enter the programme. Its a sign that HackFwd, one of the few accelerators in Europe to stress the need for technical co-founders, is opening up to new teams.
