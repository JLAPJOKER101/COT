According to the official Google Blog, Google Earth downloads have topped 1 billion since its 2005 launch. Yes, 1 billion. After acquiring Keyhole, Inc in 2005, Google was able to launch Google Earth and never imagined our geospatial technology would be used by people in so many unexpected ways, writes Brian McClendon, VP of Engineering for Google Earth and Maps. To celebrate, Google is aggregating all of the interesting ways people across the globe have used Google Earth and posting them on www.OneWorldManyStories.com.
