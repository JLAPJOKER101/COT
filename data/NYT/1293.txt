

Students in Burkina Faso demonstrated in several cities to protest the deaths of a student in police custody last month and of other marchers killed in later protests, according to a local human rights group. Demonstrations took place in Koudougou, Fada N&rsquo;Gourma, Tougan and Sabou, Chrizogone Zougmore, president of the Burkinabe Movement for Human Rights, said by phone.
