

The Chechen leader Ramzan Kadyrov testified by videolink in the Moscow trial of a prominent human rights activist he sued for defamation. The activist, Oleg Orlov, head of the rights group Memorial, had accused Mr. Kadyrov of being behind the killing of Natalya Estemirova, a Chechen rights activist. Mr. Kadyrov flippantly dismissed the accusation. &ldquo;My hands are very clean and white,&rdquo; he said. &ldquo;I had a manicure the day before yesterday.&rdquo;
