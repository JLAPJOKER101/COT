

A committee on Tuesday announced its list of four final candidates to become the chief prosecutor for the International Criminal Court: Fatou Bensouda of Gambia, Andrew Cayley of Britain, Mohamed Chande Othman of Tanzania and Robert Petit of Canada. The prosecutor is expected to be chosen in December to replace Luis Moreno-Ocampo of Argentina. Mrs. Bensouda is widely considered to be the front-runner.
