

The United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization is to announce Thursday a partnership with the Varkey GEMS Foundation to train 10,000 school principals in India, Ghana and Kenya. The foundation is the philanthropic arm of GEMS Education of Dubai, United Arab Emirates, a provider of for-profit, private education in Europe, Asia and Africa. By training principals, the program aspires to benefit thousands of teachers and &ldquo;up to 10 million children.&rdquo; The foundation will contribute $1 million toward the partnership.
