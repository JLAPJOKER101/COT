

The African Union said Friday that it would form a panel of heads of state to come up with a legally binding resolution to the leadership crisis in Ivory Coast within one month. The panel will be led by President Mohamed Ould Abdel Aziz, left, of Mauritania, which heads the union&rsquo;s Peace and Security Council. The other members will be chosen within the next two days, Mr. Abdel Aziz said.
