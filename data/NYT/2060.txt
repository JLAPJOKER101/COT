

A school warden was charged Thursday with the murder of a 7-year-old boy accused of stealing about $2.30 from a classmate, a government lawyer said. The boy, Saiful Syazani Saiful Sopfidee, lapsed into a coma after being tied to a window for two hours, caned and beaten, Deputy Public Prosecutor Azman Abu Hassan said. The warden of the private Islamic school, Hanif Mohamad Ali, 26, faces the death penalty if convicted.
