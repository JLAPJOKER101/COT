Kobo has partnered with social music discovery and streaming service Rdio to let users of its just-released Android-powered Vox eReader access more than 12 million tracks while theyre reading.Kobo, which was just acquired by Rakuten for $315 million in cash, says it boasts over five million users worldwide.Rdio is currently available in the US, Canada, and Brazil.In the United States, the Kobo Vox eReader is available in Frys stores, and available online from U.S. retailers including Best Buy, Frys and Kobo.com for $199.99.
