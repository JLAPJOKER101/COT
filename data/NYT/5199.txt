

A United Nations office said Tuesday that it was deeply worried by anti-Roma demonstrations in Bulgaria. Thousands of people have rallied since Sept. 23 to protest government handling of economic, social and ethnic problems. &ldquo;The hate speech that has been fueling the anti-Roma protests is of great concern,&rdquo; Rupert Colville, spokesman for the United Nations agency, the Office of the High Commissioner for Human Rights, said in Geneva.
