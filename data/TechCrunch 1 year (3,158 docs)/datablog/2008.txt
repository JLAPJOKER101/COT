Instead, put your query to BookLamp, a free service/project aimed at creating a database of like books. Similar to Pandora, the service is based on the Book Genome Project and attempts to find books that are similar based on measurable criteria. The project, founded in 2003, is trying to figure out the DNA of certain books by rating them based on various characteristics.Like similar music services, BookLamp gives books different ratings based on their content.
