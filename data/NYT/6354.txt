

The former chief of the Beijing airport has been sentenced to 12 years in prison for taking bribes to arrange jobs and set up corrupt business deals. The state news media said Thursday that the official, Zhang Zhizhong, was convicted in Hebei Province of receiving $740,000 in illicit payments from 1996 to 2009. His predecessor, who had also been convicted of corruption, was put to death in 2009.
