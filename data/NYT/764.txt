

Courthouses across France heard only urgent cases on Thursday as thousands of magistrates and police officers demonstrated after President Nicolas Sarkozy criticized the justice system&rsquo;s handling of a grisly murder case. Last week, Mr. Sarkozy denounced the early release from prison of the primary suspect in the killing of an 18-year-old waitress. While the magistrates agree that the system had failed,  they said it had stemmed from a lack of resources.
