

Nigerian financial regulators nationalized three banks on Friday night in the course of an inquiry into corruption and mismanagement of fiscal institutions. Finance Minister Yerima Ngama said the move &ldquo;represents an important milestone in the process of stabilizing the banks and enables these banks to move forward with a more certain future.&rdquo; The nationalizations came two years after the nation&rsquo;s central bank injected $2.55 billion into the troubled banking industry.
