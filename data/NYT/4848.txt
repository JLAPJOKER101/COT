

President Obama intends to nominate Michael McFaul, his top adviser on Russia policy and a former Stanford University professor, to be the next American ambassador to Moscow, the White House said Wednesday. Mr. McFaul advised Mr. Obama during the 2008 presidential campaign and was a leading architect of the &ldquo;reset&rdquo; of relations between the United States and Russia, one of the president&rsquo;s top foreign policy initiatives.
