

A former security contractor for Blackwater Worldwide was sentenced in Virginia on Monday to 30 months for manslaughter in the death of an Afghan man in 2009 in Kabul. The defendant, Justin Cannon, 29, and a second contractor, Christopher Drotleff, were accused of shooting two Afghans in a car at an intersection and a man walking his dog. The passenger in the car and the passer-by were killed, and the other man was wounded. Mr. Drotleff has been sentenced to 37 months in prison.
