

The United Nations says Sudan&rsquo;s north and south have agreed to withdraw all &ldquo;unauthorized forces&rdquo; from Abyei, a contested border region where fears are rising that a new conflict could ignite as Southern Sudan prepares to become an independent country. The northern and southern governments agreed to deploy a joint north-south force in Abyei and withdraw the forces that are stoking tensions in the area, the United Nations said Sunday.
