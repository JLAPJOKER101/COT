

Ilich Ramirez Sanchez, also known as Carlos the Jackal, was convicted of organizing four deadly bomb attacks in France in the 1980s and sentenced to life in prison on Thursday. Mr. Ramirez, who pleaded not guilty, was already serving a life sentence in a Paris prison for a triple murder in 1975. The court found him guilty of instigating four attacks in 1982 and 1983 that killed 11 people and wounded more than 140.
