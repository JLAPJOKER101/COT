

Philippine officials said Wednesday they had received no word from the abductors of a naturalized American woman, her teenage son and Filipino nephew a day after they were seized by gunmen. More than a dozen men seized Gerfa Yeatts Lunsmann, her 14-year-old son and 19-year-old nephew from the house of the relatives they were visiting in a village near Zamboanga city.        

&ensp;&ensp;&ensp;The area is a stronghold of the Qaeda-linked Abu Sayyaf extremists. Philippine military officials said they were also looking at the possible involvement of an Islamic separatist group.
