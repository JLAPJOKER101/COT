
China executed 12 people in a single day, the official Xinhua News Agency said on Thursday. The agency reported that Liu Zhuiheng, 52, who was convicted and sentenced to death on Thursday for detonating explosives outside a tax office in Changsha, the capital of Hunan Province, in July 2010, and 11 other people were executed. China&rsquo;s Supreme Court approved the sentences. Death sentences are sent to the court for review and are usually carried out immediately if they are approved. The 11 others were convicted of crimes including murder and robbery.
