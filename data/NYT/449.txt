

The French police on Tuesday handed over a Rwandan rebel leader to the International Criminal Court in The Hague, which wanted him on charges of rape, murder, torture and other atrocities committed during a terror campaign against civilians in the Democratic Republic of Congo&rsquo;s Kivu region. The rebel, Callixte Mbarushimana, who fled Rwanda, was living in Paris as a political refugee until the French police arrested him in October.
