

ISTANBUL &mdash; Hundreds of Turkish airstrikes and artillery assaults over the last week have killed at least 100 Kurdish separatists and injured more than 80, an army statement said Tuesday.&nbsp;        

The airstrikes, aimed at militant bases, focused on the mountainous Qandil, Hakurk, Avasin-Basyan, Zap and Metina regions, the statement posted on the military headquarters&rsquo; Web site said. &nbsp;        

&nbsp;The Turkish military said its operations would continue.
